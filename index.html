✓ What this course is

Decision-maker fluency: metrics and KPI trees, funnels, cohorts and RFM, forecasting and churn intuition, evaluation literacy, and the insight-to-action memo. Everything a leader needs to ask sharper questions and act on answers.

✗ What it is not

No coding, no SQL or Python. No deep learning, no recommendation engines, no search ranking, no GenAI - those arrive in the AI literacy course, now live. Boundaries kept on purpose so the working toolkit actually sticks.
